Madhyamakahṛdayambhavyakṛtambodhicittāparityāgaḥapratarkyamavijñeyamanālayamalakṣaṇam|anirūpyaṃ
svasaṃvedyamanādinidhanaṃ śivam

nirvikalpaṃ nirābhāsaṃ nirnimittaṃ nirañjanam |na dvayaṃ nādvayaṃ śāntaṃ dhīpracāravivarjitam

tattvaṃ dideśa kāruṇyād yo nirakṣaramakṣaraiḥ |bhāvato'stu namastasmai śāstre'vitathavādine

mahābodhau kṛtadhiyāṃ parārthodayadīkṣayā |tattvāmṛtāvatārāya śaktitaḥ kiṃciducyate

bodhicittāparityāgo munivratasamāśrayaḥ |tattvajñānaiṣaṇā ceti caryā sarvārthasiddhaye

bodhicittaṃ mahāmaitrīkaruṇājñānabhūṣaṇam |buddhabījaṃ yato vidvāṃstadatyāgāya yujyate

dhīmatā sattvamahatā paraduḥkhe'sahiṣṇunā |samyagārabdhavīryeṇa yuktaṃ śaktimatā satā

lokamālokya sakalaṃ prajñālokatiraskṛtam |saṃsārāmedhyapātālāt tīrtvā tārayituṃ svayam

kā vā śaktimataḥ saktiryadanāthāṃstapasvinaḥ |rāgādinigaḍairbaddhān ghore saṃsāracārake

pramādamadirāṃ pītvā prasuptān mohanidrayā |vitarkataskarāśeṣaviluptaśubhasaṃcayān

prajñāniśitanistriṃśacchinnaniḥśeṣabandhanaḥ |mukto na mocayedenān yadayaṃ karuṇātmakaḥ

yo'pi bāhubalopāttaṃ parīttaṃ kāmajaṃ sukham |ekākī kṛpaṇo bhuṅakte so'pi garhyaḥ satāṃ bhavet

kiṃ punaścakravartīndrabahmaṇāmapi durlabham |atyantatṛṣṇāvicchedi sādhāraṇamupāyataḥ

vigrahakṣayaparyantaduḥkhādyanabhibhāvitam |niḥśeṣaduḥkhaśamanaṃ tattvārthādhigamāmṛtam

kiṃ ca kleśagrahāveśād duḥkhaṃ duḥkhātureṣvapi |kṛtaṃ yeṣu mayā pūrvaṃ kṣatakṣāropahāravat

ye ca jātyantare premabahumānopakāriṇaḥ |teṣāṃ pratikriyā kānyā nirvāṇaprāpaṇād bhavet

na bādhyante pradīptāsikalpairapi bhavairbudhāḥ |parārthakaraṇodbhūtaprītihlāditacetasaḥ

dauḥśīlyākriyayā sarvadurgatidvārabandhanāt |śūnyatādarśanābhyāsāt kleśavṛttyupaghātataḥ

kṛtapuṇyatayodyānayātrāmiva ca ṣaḍgatim |paśyantastrāsamāyānti na santo bhavacārakāt

na bhave doṣadarśitvāt kṛpālutvānna nirvṛtau |sthitāstiṣṭhanti ca bhave parārthodayadīkṣitāḥ

bhedābhedena saṃsāranirvāṇānupalambhataḥ |na ca kvacana tiṣṭhanti sarvatra ca bhave budhāḥ

bodhyaṅgasaptaratnāḍhyaṃ labdhvāmitaguṇākaram |saddharmacakravartitvaṃ surāsuranamaskṛtam

muktābhedaśaratpūrṇaśaśāṅkakiraṇāmalaiḥ |yaśobhiśca vacobhiśca pūrayitvā diśo daśa

triratnavaṃśasthitaye sthāpayitvā guṇākarān |satputrān devanāgādicūḍāmahitaśāsanān

kṛtasvaparakāryatvānnirvṛtāste sunirvṛtāḥ |ato'nye skandhavicchedāducchinnāste'pi nirvṛtāḥ

na kroṣṭā mattamātaṅgakumbhāsphālanapāṭane |na mātaṅgaḥ samudrāntamahīmaṇḍalapālane

gambhīrānuttarodārasaṃkhyātītaguṇākare |na hīnasattbo buddhatve manorathamapīcchati

aprameyādbhutācintyasarvadīptyatibhāviṣu |saṃbuddhaguṇaratneṣu kaḥ kurvīta na mānasam

kaḥ parārthakriyāśūro mahāpuruṣaceṣṭitaiḥ |nānantamapi saṃsāraṃ tiṣṭhedekamaho yathā

kaḥ parārthaikakāryāṇāmāryāṇāṃ paddhatiṃ kṛtī |nānuyāyādatīto'pi yat kathāvastutāṃ vrajet

ko'sau parahitādhānasādhanīkaraṇāt tanum |na nayet kadalīphenaniḥsāraṃ merusāratām

pratikṣaṇaṃ jarāmṛtyurujāmāśrayamāśrayam |karoti dhanyaḥ kāruṇyāt parasaukhyodayāśrayam

sasaddharmapradīpo hi praṇaṣṭāṣṭākṣaṇaḥ kṣaṇaḥ |saphalīkaraṇīyo'yaṃ mahāpuruṣacaryayā

bodhicittāparityāgo nāma uṣaceṣṭitaiḥ |nānantamapi saṃsāraṃ tiṣṭhedekamaho yathā

kaḥ parārthaikakāryāṇāmāryāṇāṃ paddhatiṃ kṛtī |nānuyāyādatīto'pi yat kat
