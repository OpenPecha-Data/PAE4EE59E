munivratasamāśrayaḥevaṃ dṛḍhamahābodhisamādāno jagaddhite |pratirūpaṃ pratipadaṃ pratipanno
virājate

sa maitryāpratighātinyā kāruṇyena prasarpiṇā |saddharmādhigamātṛpto dharmadāne vimatsaraḥ

ṛjunāhatamānena manasā tattvadarśinā |paradoṣekṣaṇāndhena svadoṣāpattibhīruṇā

vivādalokasaṃsargalokāyataparāṅmukhaḥ |nirguṇe'pi guṇāghānapratataḥ sraṃsitādaraḥ

kṛpodbhūtena dānena sarvajñatvagataspṛhaḥ |sarvasya janakāyasya sarvaduḥkhopaśāntaye

śīlāmalajalasnāyī kṣamāsitapavitrakaḥ |vīryabaddhajaṭābhāro dhyānajñānaparāyaṇaḥ

samunmiṣitadhīnetraḥ śāstralokajñatāpaṭuḥ |hnīvyavatrāpyavasanaḥ sauratyatanubandhanaḥ

kṛpākṛṣṇājinadharaḥ śraddhāmalakamaṇḍaluḥ |smṛtiguptendriyadvāro dhṛtivetrāsanāsanaḥ

mahāyānamahāramyatapovanasamāśrayaḥ |dhyānaprītiphalāhāraḥ smṛtyupasthānagocaraḥ

gambhīrodārasūtrāntasvādhyāyahatakilbiṣaḥ |pratītyotpādasāvitrīṃ japan satyadvayāśrayāt

varṇāḍhyaiḥ pratipatpuṣpaiḥ sarvadiggandhavāhibhiḥ |upāsīnastvaharahaḥ samyaksaṃbuddhabhāskaram

hutākuśalasaṃkalpaḥ pratisaṃkhyānapāvake |munivrataṃ caredevamanuttarapadāptaye

munivratasamāśrayo nāma raddhāmalakamaṇḍaluḥ |smṛtiguptendriyadvāro dhṛtivetrāsanāsanaḥ

mahāyānamahāramyatapovanasamāśrayaḥ |dhyānaprītiphalāhāraḥ smṛtyupasthānagocaraḥ

gambhīrodārasūtrāntasvādhyāyahatakilbiṣaḥ |pratītyotpādasāvitrīṃ japan satyadvayāśrayāt

varṇāḍhyaiḥ
